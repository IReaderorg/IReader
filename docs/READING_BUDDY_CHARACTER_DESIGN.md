## ðŸ“ Design Philosophy

> "Mochi should feel like a supportive friend who genuinely cares about your reading journey. 
> Never judgmental, always encouraging, and celebrates every small victory with you."

1. **Approachable** - Soft, round shapes invite interaction

5. **Inclusive** - Appeals to all ages and backgrounds
