# General Guide

Information about supporting the project and exclusive features.

## Donation

IReader is an open-source project. If you enjoy using it, consider supporting the development.

### How to Donate
1.  Go to **Settings** → **About** → **Support Us**.
2.  You will see options for cryptocurrency donations (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.).
3.  Copy the wallet address or scan the QR code to send a donation.

## IReader NFT & Badges

We have a limited collection of NFTs that grant you a special "Verified Owner" badge in the app.

### Buying an NFT
1.  Go to **Settings** → **Badges** → **NFT Badge**.
2.  Tap **Visit Marketplace** to see available NFTs on OpenSea (or supported marketplace).
3.  Purchase an NFT using your crypto wallet.

### Verifying Ownership
Once you own an NFT:
1.  Go to **Settings** → **Badges** → **NFT Badge**.
2.  Enter your **Wallet Address** (the one holding the NFT).
3.  Tap **Verify Ownership**.
4.  If successful, you will receive the **NFT Badge** on your profile.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Verification is cached for 24 hours. You may need to re-verify occasionally.
