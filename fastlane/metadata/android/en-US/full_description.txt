IReader is a modern, feature-rich ebook reader application for Android that provides a seamless reading experience.

Features:
• Support for multiple ebook formats (EPUB, etc.)
• Customizable reading experience
• Library management
• Offline reading support
• Clean and intuitive interface
• Material Design 3
• Dark and light themes

This F-Droid version is built without proprietary dependencies, ensuring your privacy and freedom:
• No Firebase Analytics
• No Firebase Crashlytics
• No Google ML Kit (translation features not available)
• No tracking or telemetry
• 100% Free and Open Source Software (FOSS)
